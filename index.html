I make things by hand, inspired by nature and drawn by personal interests. My engineering background is a tool in that process, not a label. I am exploring different art forms while learning how to connect technical skills with craftsmanship.

About

I am a 24 year old Electrical Engineering master’s student from Belgrade. I am looking for ways to utilize software tools for repetitive phases of handmaking processes, while preserving organic essence of creative vision. My approach to craft is patient and systematic, with great attention to details.

My work spans across jewelry, clay, textile design and printmaking.
